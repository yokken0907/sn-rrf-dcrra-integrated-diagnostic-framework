# Claim Boundary

This repository is a claim-bounded diagnostic residual-response audit for SPARC galaxy rotation-curve residuals.

## Allowed interpretation

SN-RRF is supported, within the reported fitting and audit protocol, as a diagnostic framework for a scale-normalized monotone-saturating residual-response family.

The fixed A10/TVC-RRF kernel is retained as a historically motivated representative member of that family, not as a universal physical law.

## Disallowed interpretations

This repository does not establish:

- dark-matter exclusion;
- Lambda-CDM replacement;
- MOND/RAR defeat or falsification;
- Bullet-Cluster explanation;
- proof of a physical TVC mechanism;
- proof that `n = 3.258993` is a universal physical exponent;
- a Hubble-tension solution;
- independent observational validation beyond the supplied SPARC residual-audit package;
- industrial, safety-certified, or operational deployment.

## DOI interpretation

A Zenodo DOI attached to this repository should be interpreted as an archived repository-release DOI. It is not a standalone peer-reviewed article DOI unless a separate publication venue explicitly assigns such an article DOI.

## Correct short statement

SN-RRF is a dimensionless residual-response family diagnostic, not a new proven law of gravity, dark matter, or cosmology.
