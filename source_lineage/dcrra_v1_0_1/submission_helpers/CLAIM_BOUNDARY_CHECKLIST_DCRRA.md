# DCRRA Claim-Boundary Checklist

Before submission or public release, verify that the manuscript and metadata do **not** claim any of the following:

- dark-matter exclusion;
- Lambda-CDM replacement;
- MOND/RAR falsification;
- NFW-halo falsification;
- Bullet-Cluster explanation;
- particle identity;
- Hubble-tension solution;
- SN-RRF as a physical dark-matter substitute;
- standalone peer-reviewed article DOI status for a Zenodo repository archive DOI.

Allowed manuscript-level claim:

> Under the reported DCRRA protocol and eligible SPARC subset, dark-component-like rotation-curve residuals can be organized into multiple diagnostic residual regimes rather than treated as a single homogeneous category.
