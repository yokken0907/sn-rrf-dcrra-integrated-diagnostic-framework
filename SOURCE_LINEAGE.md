## Claim boundary

This complete repository does not broaden the claim boundary. It remains a claim-bounded diagnostic framework for SPARC rotation-curve residual regimes. It does not claim dark-matter exclusion, Lambda-CDM replacement, MOND/RAR falsification, NFW-halo falsification, a particle-identification inference, a Hubble-tension solution, or full non-SPARC external residual-level validation.
